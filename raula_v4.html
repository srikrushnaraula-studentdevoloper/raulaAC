My Story

I Lost a Business Because of Bad Books.

I was a young entrepreneur. My business had customers and revenue — but I had no system. Spending wasn't tracked. Dues were forgotten. Every month I assumed profit, but the real numbers told a different story.

The business didn't fail because of the market. It failed because I couldn't see where the money was going.

That lesson cost me more than money. It taught me that accounting is the basic right of every business owner. I became Tally certified and built RAULA Ac & GST — to give small and medium businesses the clarity, accuracy, and accountability I never had.
